This hardware allows the wearer to change the curvature of spacetime so that it only affects the component itself and carrying the wearer with the component. A CM is given the task of deciding the storage of a particularly large set of gravitational energy that can be stored in a point to change the curvature. The size of the storage depends on the model of the Flight Module. The Empresses’ module can manipulate a lot of energy that can accelerate to speeds higher than the speed of sound. The CM is controlled by a Pallakidan and so enables a way to steer. The speed can be controlled by how much energy is stored in the object you want to accelerate to. The GDC is also equipped with an emergency speed control system that automatically and gradually decreases the amount of gravitational energy to slow down the speed as the wearer approaches the target where the gravitation is stores. This system is excluded from wearer control so as to prevent tampering with this system by anyone. The GDC includes a protection system for the wearer against the heat. This system decreases the movement of immediate particles around the field that has been generated and so will make a shell of condensation whenever someone flies in a flight module. In particularly cold areas when flight speed isn’t much, it will work to maintain a hotter temperature. In short, the system will keep the immediate area around the wearer at room temperature. The attachment of the GDC is different than the hardware components of other armor. The GDC is tied to the back. This allows for a more stronger hold so that when the component is pulled towards the stored gravitational point, the GDC securely pulls the wearer with it.

Clothing

This is the second part of the Flight Module that contains the armor for protection, wind deflector field to give more speed and, of course, the clothing which covers the Empresses’ naked body. The clothing is, as always, a midriff but there are more layers underneath. The first layer is a thick midriff that cushions the naked body from the scratchy hardware of the hardware midriff. Over this midriff goes a hardware midriff that contains the wind deflector field and armor mechanism for the upper body. The wind deflector mechanism is a mechanism that maintains a mechanical field through a CM. This field helps protect the wearer from pressure attained by high speeds. The field is made in such a way that it even allows a boost to the speed of the module. Also on the hardware midriff is a armor component. This armor component only contains a mechanical field that deflects weapons and is not fully effective against power weapons as the MEC system. While the wind deflector field component is attached to front of the midriff right on the sides of the breast, the armor component is attached to the upper back above the GDC. Over the hardware midriff is the black outer midriff. This midriff is very thick and is a cushioning padding covered by the same tight and stretchy material in the ceremonial wear. The back of this midriff is extended to the pants so that some of the wires in the midriff can be connected to the pants. The midriff is sleeveless. The pants also have three layers like the upper body clothing. The first layer is cushioning pants that protects the body from the scratchy hardware layer. The second layer is the hardware layer. This layer only consists of a multitude of wirings that enable the extension of the hardware previously talked about. Over the hardware layer is pants that also have a padding covered by a stretchy and tight material. These pants are black and there is a black belt with a silver buckle. Holsters meant to hold guns are strapped near the crotch. A cloth headgear is worn for the main purpose of hiding the comm system. The comm system basically uses electromagnetic waves to relay information to a receiving point. Common EM comm system use lower frequency as they are easier to detect. The Air Force and Armed Force use a higher frequency, higher than the frequency of visible light. The Prime Force uses a frequency higher than the Air Force and Armed Force. A mike is put near the mouth underneath the cloth headgear while the receiver end is placed near the ears. The boots are specially made so that keep it keeps a lot of heat.

Reconnaissance Module#

Description

This is a very nimble armor, mostly used by spies. The Empresses use this armor when they don’t want to get noticed in the army. It is mostly used by them when they want the enemy to know that they are somewhere and then disappear from there and so not actually being there.

Cognitive Hardware

The Cognitive Hardware, in the Reconnaissance Module, or the Recon Module, is the LD component. The LD component, or the Light Deflector component, allows the wearer to deflect any electromagnetic radiation that is coming towards them. There are many parts to this ability. The first and foremost of the component’s ability is to deflect any light whatsoever coming to the wearer. The light is deflected precisely when it hits a field that is right around the edge of the wearer. A CM in the module takes the shape of the person using the different energies located in the body to create a field around the wearer. The light, coming from any direction is swept aside from the field, around the wearer. The logic behind this is very simple. The field that the CM creates has two gravitational layers. The gravity that is directed by the bottom layer is directed away from the wearer while the gravity of the top layer is directed in towards the wearer. The light is bent by the gravity and because of opposing forces of the layer stays directly in the middle of the two layers. The light molds around the wearer and then passes out. The direction of each and every ray is kept straight by thousands of small CM in the module working every moment. These smaller CM only have one objective, which is keeping the light straight. The same CM that creates the gravitation field is also assigned the job of conforming the field to the movement of the wearer. There is a limit to the area of the field. The module of the Empresses has the capacity to only allow one being to be hidden. Another limit of the module is separate objects. An external object that a wearer carries will be included in the field and disappear from any watcher but will reappear when the wearer releases it. A machine that works by sending information by electromagnetic radiation will be disrupted by the field around the wearer, may it be a friendly device for communication or a non-friendly device to track the wearer. The signal though may be caught by accident to anyone with the proper receiver.

Clothing

The clothing of the Recon Module has three parts to it just like that of the Flight Module. The first part is the armor, which is similar to the one used for the light armor, the communication system or the comm, and, of course, the actual clothing covering the Empresses’ naked body. The clothing is as always a midriff, although this midriff is sleeveless, with pants and a cloth headgear with slits in it for the eyes. The midriff, like the Flight Module has three layers. The first, innermost layer is a soft layered cushioning against the hardware layer. The hardware layer contains the hardware for the armor and the comm. The top layer is a thick layer to hide the hardware layer with a stretchy material. The bottom is the same thing: the first layer is a cushioning soft layered midriff over which is the hardware layer over which is another midriff made of a stretchy material. The hardware layer for the pants, like the Flight Module, is only wires for extensions of the hardware on the hardware midriff. The topmost midriff has an extension in the back that allows the wires to connect with the hardware layer in the pants. A black belt with a silver buckle goes over the pants. The shoes are specially made of very soft material as to make less sound. The cloth headgear is made of cloth and has slits for the eyes. It extends all the way down the neck to the neckline of the midriff. The armor hardware is the same powered armor fitted in the Flight Module. It is slightly modified so as to not make the crackling sound it usually emits when hit. The armor is fitted on the sides of the breasts in the hardware layer. The comm is a special system that is designed for the Recon Module. Since electromagnetic waves like radio waves cannot be used for communication, the comm system uses high frequency sound waves to communicate. Since sound waves said by any wearer is not high frequency, the CM in the system translate everything spoken by the wearer into a language that uses a higher frequency. The message is sent and then converted quickly at the station of communication. There are three types of high frequency languages used to send messages with this Module. The first one is a common messaging language available to anyone, the second is a military language used by only the Air Force and Armed Force, the third is a highly confidential language used by only the Prime Force. The Recon Module of the Empresses is equipped with only the Prime Force messaging language. The Comm system is located at the back of the neck and is connected with a mike under the cloth headgear. Another headpiece where the messages are received is placed on the ears under the cloth headgear.

Armor Module#

Description

The Heavy Module is a heavy super soldier armor used to help fight against tanks. It cannot infiltrate small places and requires a lot of material and so only a few in the army has them including the Empresses, and the Goddesses. These Heavy Modules can pack a large amount of firepower and so are valuable assets in an open field.

Cognitive Hardware

The Cognitive Hardware for the Heavy and Multi Purpose Module is as important for the armor as the clothing. This is a strange case in modules because unlike other Modules, Heavy and Multi Purpose Modules don’t only depend on their Cognitive Hardware to give them their function. The Cognitive Hardware for the Heavy Armor is a armor for deflecting the powered parts of any attack. They are not meant to withstand any physical attacks. But, the power armor of the Heavy Armor is a lot more effective than normal power armor used by regular soldiers. The Heavy Armor Cognitive Hardware is called Magnetic Electrical Component or the MEC system. The MEC system is a system that uses a combination of magnetic fields to slow down and repel the electrical fields around the edges of a power weapon. Although not in the name, a mechanical field is also used as protection against the actual weapon. There are three CM in the component: one controls the electrical field, another controls the magnetic field and one that controls the mechanical field. All three fields are equally important in the Module to maximize the protection against power weapons. The magnetic field is used to slow down and deflect the weapon. The electrical field is also used as a way to repel the weapon as specific electrons will try to move away from each other. The electrical field isn’t equally strong in all sides. Some points of the electrical field are stronger than others. The magnetic field then comes into play as it deflects each attack onto the strong point, away from the weak point. The mechanical field is equally layered over the surface area of the armor. It deflects the actual metal inside the weapon as neither the electrical field or the Red Energy are affected by it. The whole surface area of the armor are equally protected by this arrangement. The CM of their corresponding field regularly change their fields to match any changes made to the armor. The armor does not fully protect against a weapon, especially launched with a powerful force. In these matters, the weight and speed of the weapon counts as much as the power of the weapon itself.

Clothing

The clothing is as important as the MEC system. Not all weapons encountered are power weapons. And in the MEC system only the mechanical field provides support against those which is not sufficient. The clothing of the Heavy Armor includes a huge suit of metal armor that is specially made to counter tanks and their weapons. The suit includes a component that allows the suit to pick up heavier material and withstand more damage. It can also do complicated analysis of fields and provide accurate information about the field. The armor functions by itself. The wearer is connected to the armor but does not have to exert any force to move it. The armor itself is double the height of any average Pallakidan. It is a full body armor and keeps a general Pallakidan shape. Most components are placed in the back of the armor. The helmet has a visor made up of thick glass with an inbuilt HUD display. Besides that, it includes a comm system for the wearer. The build of the armor is quite slim in proportion with the size of the armor. The pieces smoothly blend into each other so that when the armor is worn the individual pieces cannot be distinguished by themselves. The chestplate conforms to the chest in that it rises out to show the breast and then drops to the stomach. The armor on the arm are slim, like that of an average Pallakidan, but contain a lot of strength. The oversized weapons of the heavy armor go on the belt while few are situated in the back. The leg armor also contains an in-built holster that reveals the guns when directed. Otherwise the guns are hidden inside the armor. The legs of the armor is like the arms in that they have an increased strength but are slim like that of a Pallakidan. The Pallakidan inside the armor does not have much room but enough so that it can break its connection for a moment and do something else. A cooling system is placed inside the armor to keep cool the machine and the Pallakidan itself. Usually the Pallakidan inside does not wear any clothing as it interferes with movement in the constricted space. The inside of the armor is comfortable enough so that there is no uncomfortable or uneasiness.

Multi Purpose Module#

Description

The Multi Purpose Module is a module containing a small part of every type of armor that the Empresses have. Although each part isn’t as effective as in its original module, they are all in one place and so making them easily accessible. The Multi Purpose Module is used in interplanetary meetings during war time.

Cognitive Hardware

The Multi Purpose Module uses a smaller version of the MEC system called the MEC Delta. It has the same same concept of the MEC system except for slight modifications to help with agility. While the actual armor does allow quick movement, the CM in the MEC system cannot respond to quick movement. To deal with this the mechanical component in the MEC system is taken off. The reduction of energy usage allows for quicker movement. To improve speed, a modified version of the wind deflector mechanism is also used. Since the most of the clothing does not offer much protection, the CM in the MEC Delta are allowed to use more energy than in MEC. MEC Delta also allows variable energy, as the user can change the energy consummation instantly during battle field, something that few modules allow. The Multi Purpose Module also contains a LD Delta that can be activated any time by the user. This version of the LD can be used to hide indirectly in the dark and is not as powerful as the one in the Reconnaissance Module. The only system from the other modules that is not included is the GDC as that module uses too much energy.

Clothing

The clothing of the Multi Purpose Module is very important although not as much. The clothing of the Multi Purpose Module is a combination of the traditional clothing and a more protective Armor style clothing. The pants are made of a black soft cloth. Over this is a hardware layer that only contains extension wires. Over the hardware layer is a thicker more stretchy layer to hide the hardware. A belt with a silver buckle connects the pants and the top. The top is a different matter. It is a large breastplate that is thick and conforms to the breast. The MEC system is placed on the back, while the LD is placed on the side of the breasts of the breastplate. The breastplate is thick and does not expose the midriff. This is because of the lack of a mechanical field in the armor module. The arms are kept exposed by this midriff. There is nothing usually worn underneath the breastplate. A helmet with a visor and an inbuilt HUD display is used as headgear. Besides that, the headgear also includes the Comm system. The shoes are made of the softest material to make as little noise as possible. The breastplate itself doesn’t weigh as much to keep the Module agile. The Multi Purpose Module is made to be a hit and run shoot that keeps protection against weapons but isn’t meant for a direct hand to hand fight.

Ceremonial#

Description

The ceremonial dress is the dress that the Empresses wear in military parades in the public. They also wear it in military special parties for the celebration after a war or battle. This is also the standard military wear. Most professional soldiers that are currently in the army are required to wear this in any formal function may it be martial or not.

Clothing

The ceremonial dress, considering that it is worn at all formal function, is very revealing. It follows the code of having the midriff open. On the top is a black midriff that is made of a very tight and stretchy material that shows a lot of breast. There is a chain in the front to take off or put on the midriff. It has a very high collar so that a Zenian would have a very stiff neck if it was chained to the very top. By an informal law, the chain is to be kept open enough to show a tiny amount of cleavage. The midriff has no sleeves. Medals line the right side on the top of the breast. The bottom is a black form fitting pant made of the same material. A holster is strapped near the crotch that carries guns. The shoes are heavy military shoes that extend all the way to the knee. The pants are neatly tucked inside the high boots. There is a special place for knives on the side of the boots that are concealed until used.

Weapons#

Empress Iasedi’s Weapons#

The primary weapon for Iasedi is two axes. These axes are power weapons, one of the deadliest of all since these weapons use a less diluted Red Energy. The axes have a short handle made of metal but is covered with a grip near the bottom. Each of the axes are double bladed with black etchings of the Iasedi’s personal insignia, the silhouette of a gliding riesis with two double bladed battle axes in its claws and siusis bordering it, saluting on the blades. The blades are of crescent shape and thick near the base where they connect with the handle. They get thinner at the very end, so thin that they can slip through almost anything. The end of the handle curves into a spike that is rarely used. The axes are made of the strongest Pallakidan made metal available in Adasi.

Empress Saiketas’s Weapons#

The primary weapon for Saiketas is two slim, flexible blades. These blades, that Saiketas can handle very well, are power weapons, equal in strength to the axes of Iasedi and the spear of Sakatha. The hilt is made of the same grip as that of Iasedi’s axes and Sakatha’s spear. The pommel itself is made out of a precious stone mined from the meteor Ias Edirao. Each of the blade’s hilt has a pommel with the personal insignia of Saiketas on the hilt, a black mountain with a crown on the tip. The crown is a golden band with black and blue jewels embedded in circlet. No crossguard adorns the blade. The blade itself is slim and not made to parry large weapons. The blade is thick where it meets the hilt and curves to an extremely sharp point near the end. The swords are made with a backbone that is extremely flexible, though when thrusted at the enemy does not bend at all. The swords are made of the strongest Pallakidan made metal available in Adasi.

Empress Sakatha’s Weapons#

The primary weapons for the Sakatha is a long bladed power spear, and a special shield. The power spear is a long pole of a very strong metal, while the long blade is about a third of the size of the pole itself and have the same power as the swords of Saiketas and the axes of Iasedi. The blade is thin and has a crescent shape, but with a straight point at the very end to make thrusts easier. The shield is a large circular shield that curves for deflection and uses a Module similar to the MEC system but stronger. The shield is colored with the personal insignia of Sakatha, a churning sea with her spear in the middle, crackling with power, and on the bottom is a crown. The crown is a silver band with black and red jewels embedded in the circlet.

Empresses’ Secondary Weapons#

The secondary weapon for all the Empresses is two guns. These guns have powered bullets and are one of the most accurate in Adasi. They are black and small with long barrels. A laser pointer is attached underneath the barrel for better aiming.
