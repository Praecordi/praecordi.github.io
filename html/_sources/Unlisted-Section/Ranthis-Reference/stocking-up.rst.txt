===========================
Stocking Up on Orgasms [1]_
===========================


A Reminder of Gems
==================

Across the landscapes of Rankvah are countless crystals called “Empty Gems”. Empty Gems are, at their simplest, minerals that can magically store and/or release energies or properties.

:doc:`Full post about the magic system <magic-system>`

Storing Orgasms
===============

When someone wants to store an orgasm for future use (You’ll understand what this means later), they’d want to use one of their own Control Gems ready. To store an orgasm, they need to have their Control Gem already inside their vagina. This prevents the user from experiencing an orgasm, though they would still experience a refractory period. Any semen that would’ve been ejaculated normally instead magically disappears, and any vaginal fluids that would’ve normally spilled also magically disappear. The orgasm is now contained inside the Control Gem, and expires after a month.

Catch and Release
=================

When someone wants to release an orgasm they have stored, the process is very simple. All you must do is take a Control Gem that already has an orgasm stored inside, place it into your mouth, and break it with your teeth. Control Gems containing orgasms break very easily inside people’s mouths, like they were chocolate, but only inside people’s mouths. The moment the gem breaks, it melts and dissipates into the user’s mouth, at which moment the orgasm is released. All of the stored semen is ejaculated, all of the vaginal fluids are expelled, and the orgasm is felt at a much greater intensity than it would’ve been felt had it been just experienced normally.

Why Go Through All the Effort?
==============================

In Rankvah, you’ll find that many people like having a spare orgasm every now and then, perhaps even mixed with another orgasm. The only thing that beats one good orgasm is multiple at the same time, so rest assured that your partner wouldn’t at all mind the extra traces you leave inside them, or on them. After enough trial and error, each person can find their optimal number of simultaneous orgasms they can experience without incapacitating themselves for hours on end.

How Can a Male or Female Otherworlder Enjoy this too?
=====================================================

As written in an earlier post, otherworlders are admired and always welcomed by anyone and everyone. You can trust that the literal entire pantheon would be more than willing to hook you up with a penis or vagina, in case you want one. If not, they won’t have a problem making an exception for you to allow storing orgasms through different parts of your body.
