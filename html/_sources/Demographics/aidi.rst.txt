.. [#f1] So here's how I'm going to determine the personality of `aiɖi`. This `website <http://ideonomy.mit.edu/essays/traits.html>`__ lists 638 traits divided into three loose categories: positive, neutral and negative. Thus as a loose characterization of each `aiɖi`, pick one trait from each category. Note that these traits are not the only traits of these `aiɖi`, but they are the defining trait of each `aiɖi`. Furthermore, the positive and negative categories will be allotted five extra slots as secondary traits. 

    For the minor `aiɖi`, we will only define primary positive, neutral and negative personality trait, and 1 extra positive and negative personality trait that will come from a major `aiɖi` ancestor.
