Zean Glossary
=============

For each base, we will give a general meaning of the base [#f1]_, the singular nominative inflection of the base and its meaning, and the 1st person simple singular present conjugation and its meaning.

Words
-----

.. glossary::
  :sorted:
  
  `aiɖi` : aidi
    a being of the ethereal realm

    - `aiɖisi` - I am a being from the ethereal realm
    - `aiɖisa` - A/the being of the ethereal realm

  `isiʈeθɶ` : isitetho
    the title of `ajɛseɖi`, `saeθa`, `saiʃeʈa` and later `iɖe`; synonymous to a ruler

    - `isiʈeθɶsi` - I rule
    - `isiʈeθɶsa` - A/the ruler

  `zœeɖaʃi` : zoedashi
    the title given to `seθi`, `aʃaða`, `teʃi`, ...; synonymous to a governor

    - `zœeɖaʃisi` - I govern
    - `zœeɖaʃisa` - a/the governor

  `aʋjasi` : avyasi
    a class of changed humans and `zea` that occurs after corruption from a Creation Ritual

    - `aʋjasisi` - I am an `aʋjasi`
    - `aʋjasisa` - an `aʋjasi`

  `ʃɛʈaie` : shataie
    the title given to the daughters of `ajɛseɖi` and `stɹai`

    - `ʃɛʈaiesi` - I am a `ʃɛʈaie` sister
    - `ʃɛʈaiesa` - a `ʃɛʈaie` sister

  `zea` : zea
    resident of the `zai` system

    - `zeasi` - I live in the `zai` system; I am a zean
    - `zeasa` - a/the resident of the `zai` system; a/the zean

  `ʈeaθyɖa` : teathuda
    a race of `aɖasi`

    - `ʈeaθyɖasi` - I am of the `ʈeaθyɖa` race
    - `ʈeaθyɖasa` - a/the being of the `ʈeaθyɖa` race

  `astɹœ` : astro

    - `tœɹtɹœsi` - I am in the `tœɹtɹœ` region *or* I am the `tœɹtɹœ` asteroid [#f4]_

    - `diʃietisjɛsa` - the city of `diʃietisjɛ`
